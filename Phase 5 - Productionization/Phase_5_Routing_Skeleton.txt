Phase 5 ‚Äî Productionization & Offline Learning Routing Skeleton
===============================================================

Purpose:
- Define the **offline learning dataflow order** across Phase 5 systems.
- Provide governance for how learning-facing layers connect.
- This is NOT a runtime decision engine.

Non-Negotiable Rules:
- Phase 5 operates offline only.
- Phase 5 MUST NOT modify any Completed Phases.
- Phase 5 outputs are introduced via deployment only.
