## Phase 5 — Curator Gold Layer

---

### Relationship to Phase 6

Curator Gold & Labeling operates **downstream of Phase 6**.

- Phase 6 governs whether execution is allowed.
- Phase 5 Curator Gold governs how outcomes are evaluated and learned from.

Gold labels:
- are not enforcement signals,
- are not routing inputs,
- and never participate in runtime decision‑making.

They exist solely to improve future models through offline learning.
