# narrative_output.interface.md

## Purpose

Defines the **final narrative output** contract produced in Phase 2
(Stage 6 – Track D).

This is the presentation-ready tips text.

---

## Shape

Narrative output includes:

- `paragraph_1` (string)
- `paragraph_2` (string)

---

## Semantics

- Paragraph structure is fixed.
- Tone is advisory and practical.
- Word budgets are enforced upstream.

---

## Guarantees

- Output is safe for UI rendering.
- No markup or executable content is present.

---

## Notes

- Localization and personalization occur in Phase 4.5+.
