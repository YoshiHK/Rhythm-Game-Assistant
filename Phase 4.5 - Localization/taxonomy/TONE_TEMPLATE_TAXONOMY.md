# Tone Template Taxonomy

## Positioning

Defines **tone overlays** applied to localized text.

Tone modifies **style, not meaning**.

---

## Non-Negotiable Boundary

Tone MUST NOT:

- alter meaning
- add new information
- remove semantic content

---

## Design Principles

1. Presentation-only  
2. Post-processing layer  
3. Always reversible  
4. Cross-language consistent  

---

## Taxonomy

### 1. Neutral

- neutral  

Describes:
- baseline tone
- no stylistic modification

---

### 2. Casual

- casual  

Describes:
- softer, more approachable tone

---

### 3. Expert

- expert  

Describes:
- concise, technical tone

---

### 4. Analytical

- analytical  

Describes:
- observational, structural tone

---

## Application Order

template → text → tone → final output

Tone is ALWAYS applied last.

---

## Summary

Tone templates ensure **consistent stylistic variation**
without affecting system correctness.
