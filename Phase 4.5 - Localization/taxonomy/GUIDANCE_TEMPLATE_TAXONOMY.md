# Guidance Template Taxonomy

## Positioning

Defines **guidance framing templates** used to express
attention direction and risk framing.

This layer is **not instructional**.

---

## Non-Negotiable Boundary

Guidance MUST NOT:

- provide step-by-step instructions
- dictate player actions
- introduce new gameplay meaning

Guidance ONLY:

> frames attention and risk.

---

## Design Principles

1. Non-instructional  
2. Attention-focused  
3. Cross-game safe  
4. Complementary to element-level

---

## Taxonomy

### 1. Primary Focus

- primary_focus  

Describes:
- main area of attention

---

### 2. Secondary Focus

- secondary_focus  

Describes:
- supporting attention areas

---

### 3. Awareness Framing

- awareness_framing  

Describes:
- attention distribution across elements

---

### 4. Risk Accumulation

- risk_accumulation  

Describes:
- how errors or instability accumulate

---

## Relationship

- Element = WHAT  
- Section = WHEN  
- Chart = OVERALL  
- Guidance = WHERE TO FOCUS  

---

## Summary

Guidance templates provide **safe attention framing**
without violating semantic constraints.
